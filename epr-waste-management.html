Recycling for a Greener World

Product Take-Back & E-Waste Recycling Programme.

NMRM Infotech's e-waste take-back and recycling service adheres to the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016 issued by the Ministry of Environment and Forest, Government of India. When your IT equipment reaches the end of its life, we take it back and recycle it — at no cost to you.

Our Pledge
To act responsibly towards the environment, NMRM Infotech has pledged to recycle Centralised Data Processing equipment — Mainframes and Minicomputers (ITEW1) that are intended to be discarded as waste by the consumer. When an electronic product reaches the end of its life, we take it back and recycle it. All NMRM Infotech products are RoHS compliant.

We believe that saving the environment is a shared responsibility between us and our consumers. Our Product Take-Back & Recycling Programme is an e-waste management initiative towards reducing the environmental impact of our products. Join us as we venture to save the environment.

FAQs

Common questions.

Do I have to pay any fee to recycle my product?

No. Consumers do not have to pay any fee to recycle products sold in India under this programme.

Will I get any monetary benefit or discount for returning unused products?

Yes. Eligible products returned through this take-back and recycling programme qualify for a monetary benefit. Our hotline team will confirm details when you call.

Who handles the actual recycling?

Recycling is performed by our Producer Responsibility Organisation partner, R2 PRO Pvt. Ltd., which is registered with the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) for EPR implementation. The full chain of custody is traceable under E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016.

Is my data safe?

You are responsible for wiping data prior to handover. NMRM Infotech, its affiliates, agents, and representatives are not responsible for the breach or loss of any data left on returned equipment. We recommend secure data destruction before dropping off any equipment.

Are NMRM Infotech products compliant with environmental standards?

Yes. All NMRM Infotech products are RoHS compliant — meeting the Restriction of Hazardous Substances directive that limits the use of specific hazardous materials in electrical and electronic equipment.
